About Us:

As a group of students from NCI and independent learners ourselves, we were tasked with the creation of a website offering tips and suggestions on self-study. While it has always been present in the academic world, self-study has become more and more popular among students due to the access to information available on the internet. However, because we were not necessarily trained during our formal education to be independent learners, it can be difficult for some people to develop such skills on their own. Taking that in consideration, we have developed this website to aid others on this journey of self-discovery and learning autonomy.
