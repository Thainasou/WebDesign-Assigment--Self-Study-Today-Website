Key benefits of the courses are:

Flexibility

The benefit is i can pick a study, time that is a convenient for me and not having to stick to a time table of others.

Contents

A good course will have a content prepared by qualified teachers and adapted and improved by student feedback.

Practicality

I can start with a beginers course and add modules to progress as and when i have time and money.

Variety

Online courses range from hobby interestes to full blown professional training. There is something avaible for everyone.

Cost benefit

The fee for an online course is competitive and less than a college fee you can purchase modules when you have saved up the cost.

Certification

Important to check that the qualification at the end of the online course is recognised by a prospective employer.
